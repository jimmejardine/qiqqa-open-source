Daniel Naumenko 1 year ago

Recaptcha prompt issue

So whenever I use the sniffer feature, inevitably it prompts me to do the standard "prove you're not a robot" recaptcha. But unlike on my outside-of-qiqqa browsing experience, the prompt is blank and there is nothing for me to click, no box to check, no words to type, thus preventing me from non-manually entering my citations. Is there any work around for this?
7878006

    tedperl 1 year ago
    The best response would be for Qiqqa to update the browser wrapped inside, but since Qiqqa orphaned the product a while ago that will *never* happen--and yet they still take my money.

    Best work around I have seen is:

    Go to google scholar on a separate browser.
    2. Search for the article.
    3. In search results click on double quotes " under the article (Between star sign and Cited by links).
    4. Click on BibTex (open in new window if you like).
    5. Copy the BibTex info and paste it manually in Qiqqa > BibTex info screen (see attached image).
    6. Hit the thumbs up button

    Andy Hegedus 1 year ago
    I tried the above and it worked. I also tried the same idea but within the sniffer and it worked too. What I mean is I used the sniffer to find the article. Then, rather than click the import to BibTex, I clicked on the double quotes. Scrolled down to the BibTex link. When I clicked on that link it populated the sniffer BibTex window. I clicked on the Thumbs up button and was done. Yay!!!
