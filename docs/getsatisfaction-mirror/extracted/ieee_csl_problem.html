IEEE CSL problem

Dear Qiqqa Programmers,
I'm a Persian user of your awesome PDF manager program. 2 years ago after a careful comparison between Qiqqa and Mendeley I chose Qiqqa, but recently I've faced a terrible problem during writing my proposal. It's about IEEE in line citation. Numbers in Persian are written in a different way from English and when I use a citation in my Persian text the numbers doesn't follow the language and they are still in English. I know that it's a problem restricted to my right-to-left language handwriting and it's a problem that I also have when using MS word citation tool. Surprisingly Mendeley works perfectly well. I searched through Internet and I found that Mendeley has had this problem before but apparently they have fixed this bug. I tried to edit csl file of IEEE in order to make it compatible but I wasn't successful. Since I have a limited time to prepare my proposal, it's an emergency for me to find a way to solve this problem and I will be very grateful to you if you could direct me to the solution.

    Syavash Asgari over 5 years ago
    Issue solved! Seemed to be a Word problem. Just highlight the number of the reference in the text (only one of the references!) and click on the downward arrow on the down-right corner of the "Style" panel.
    On the drop-down list, click on the last option "Apply Styles...". Then click "Modify..." on the new panel on the up-left corner of the screen. Now one should change font under "Formatting" and set it to something like B Nazanin.

    Goodluck
