Watch this excellent tutorial, courtesy Salve Regina University Library.

Step 2.

Get yourself a Qiqqa account. This will allow you to sync your documents (if you like), and join other Libraries. Then log in to this website with that account.

If you run into any trouble, check out our help resources, or if you have any questions or comments, please feel free to drop us a line.
