Highlighting is nonfunctional

The highlighting feature is not very good - I will often spend a minute or two just trying to highlight a single line. I try to drag along a line to highlight it and I end up highlight the lines above and below it. When I try to un-highlight the unintended lines, it un-highlights the lines I want to keep too. The highlighting tool is simply to hard to use, and often highlight unintentional items.

I would absolutely love it if the highlighting was as capable as Adobe Reader - that works so well and the colors are so vibrant. The colors in Qiqqa are really bland (yes I have the Premium Version and I still think the colors are too light and old looking.

    Qiqqa (Official Rep) over 7 years ago
    The highlighting feature is a function of how well the PDF was OCRed. If the PDF comes with electronic text embedded in it, highlighting should be almost perfect. If it requires OCRing (e.g. if the page is scanned) then the word detection may not be as good as desired.

    If you think you have a PDF that is behaving particularly badly, pls send an example to support@qiqqa.com so we can look at it.

    First, thanks for the great product, Qiqqa is fantastic.

Also in Adobe, you select a text and it is only highlighted when you release the cursor, which gives chance to correct your selection if needed. In Qiqqa the highlighting is made immediately. Personally, I would prefer the Adobe-style highlighting.
