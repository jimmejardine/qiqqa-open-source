    Hi Mauricio.

    When you say "I'm the administrator in either qiqqa or word" - do you mean you're running both as administrator?

    Qiqqa (Official Rep) over 7 years ago
    While we look for a more general solution, this has worked for a few other Qiqqa users. It seems that the uninstall of Word 2013 must have removed something else from your computer to make Word 2010 no longer work.

    We have found this:
    http://stackoverflow.com/questions/12...

    If you feel comfortable in the registry, could you please open
    regedit.exe

    then open HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\TypeLib\{00020905-0000-0000-?????}
    is there an 8.5 and an 8.6?

    We suspect that if you remove the 8.6 item, it will all work again...

    Cheers,
    Qiqqa Support
